About MediPredict AI

Discover the technology powering our machine-learning symptoms diagnostic engine.

Random Forest Classifier

Our core machine learning module uses an ensemble **Random Forest Classifier** trained on high-dimensional clinical symptom vector spaces. When you select your symptoms, they are encoded as a binary vector (1 for present, 0 for absent) representing 132 possible symptoms.

The model passes this vector through an ensemble of 100 decision trees. Each tree makes a classification vote based on the features, and the system aggregates these votes to predict the final prognosis with an calculated probability index (confidence score).

Data Integrity

Trained on a robust dataset matching 132 different symptoms across 41 unique infectious and systemic diseases, including respiratory, cardiovascular, digestive, and dermatological conditions.

Specialist Routing

Based on the predicted disease, the system automatically runs database lookups to direct you to the correct class of medical professional (e.g., Dermatologist, Pulmonologist, Hepatologist).

Important Educational Disclaimer

MediPredict AI is an educational technology demonstration. It does not replace physical laboratory analyses, clinical assessments, or direct advice from certified general practitioners. Do not use this tool as a final diagnosis for treating severe acute illnesses.
